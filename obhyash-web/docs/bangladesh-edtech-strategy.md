# Bangladesh EdTech Market Analysis — Obhyash Strategic Brief

> Generated: March 7, 2026

---

## 1. The Market Reality

Bangladesh has one of the most **exam-obsessed student cultures in the world**. Everything revolves around 4 high-stakes bottlenecks:

| Exam                          | Students/Year                | Anxiety Level | Willingness to Pay          |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------- | ------------- | --------------------------- |
| SSC                           | ~2M                          | High          | Low (parents control money) |
| HSC                           | ~1.4M                        | Very High     | Medium                      |
| Medical Admission (MBBS)      | ~130K compete for ~11K seats | Extreme       | Very High                   |
| Engineering (BUET/7 Colleges) | ~80K compete for ~6K seats   | Extreme       | Very High                   |

**Key insight**: 95% of revenue in BD edtech comes from the top 10% of students — the ones desperate to get into medical or BUET. That's your primary audience to win first.

### Market Tailwinds

- Mobile data is now ৳1/day in bundles — every student is online
- bKash/Nagad has made digital payments normal for students
- COVID permanently normalized online learning
- Coaching center fees (৳15,000–৳50,000/year) make ৳499 look trivial
- ~3.5M students are in HSC annually — massive TAM

## 2. How the Big Players Actually Win Students

### Udvash (Market Leader)

- **Brand = trust signal**: 30+ year old coaching center. Parents recognize the name.
- Hook: Free admission seminars in schools → teacher recommends → parent pays
- Digital is an **add-on** to their offline presence, not the core
- They don't need product-market fit — they have brand-market fit
- **You can't compete here directly**

### Chorcha.net

- Pure content play: Notes + MCQs, organized by chapter/topic
- Monetizes with **annual bundles per subject group** (SSC Pack, HSC Pack)
- SEO-first: Ranks #1 for "HSC Physics MCQ" type searches
- Students come for free notes, stay for practice, convert on urgency (exam approaching)
- **Weakness**: Zero personalization, no analytics, feels like a PDF website
- **No community, no gamification**

### 10 Minute School (Ayman Sadiq)

- **Virality engine**: YouTube-first, then app
- Free video content drives millions to platform
- Converts 2–5% to paid courses
- Brand = Ayman Sadiq personally. Students trust the face, not the product.
- **Weakness**: No adaptive practice, no real assessment engine

### Shikho

- VC-funded, heavy on video + MCQ
- Structured learning paths per subject
- Push notification heavy — daily reminders
- **Weakness**: Expensive (৳600–৳1200/month), feels corporate, not student-native

### ACS (Academy of Science)

- Physics/Chemistry/Math specialist coaching
- Digital presence is secondary
- Students go there for teacher reputation, not product

> **Common pattern across all**: They win through **either brand trust OR free content volume**. Not product excellence.

## 4. Student Psychology in Bangladesh

Understanding this is more important than any feature:

### Fear of Missing Out on a Seat > Everything Else

The medical seat scarcity (1 in 12 chance) means students will pay anything that reduces perceived risk. Frame every feature around **"এতে কতটা chance বাড়বে?"** (How much does this improve my chances?)

### Social Proof Beats Product Demo

"আমার বন্ধু এটা দিয়ে 85% পেয়েছে" converts better than any feature list. You need student testimonials and rank improvement stories from day one.

### Exam Date Creates Urgency

Students are passive until T-60 days before exam. Then they become frantic buyers. Build your marketing calendar around MBBS admission date, HSC exam date, etc.

### Parents Pay, Students Decide

- **Pitch to parents**: "coaching-এর চেয়ে সস্তা, যেকোনো সময় পড়তে পারবে" (cheaper than coaching, study anytime)
- **Pitch to students**: leaderboard, streaks, competitiveness

### Mobile-Only Audience

70%+ of your users will never open a laptop. Every feature must work perfectly on a ৳8,000 Android phone on 3G.

## 5. Subscription Model

### The Problem with the Current Model

`৳149/month` is psychologically wrong for this market.

Students don't think in months — they think in **exam sessions**. A student preparing for MBBS 2026 needs ~6 months of serious prep. A monthly subscription means they have to consciously re-buy 6 times, each time feeling the money leave. **Annual or session-based pricing removes that friction entirely.**

### Market Benchmarks

| Platform         | Pricing                                          |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| Chorcha          | ~৳350 for an HSC annual bundle per subject group |
| Shikho           | ৳700–৳1200/month                                 |
| Coaching centers | ৳15,000–৳40,000/year                             |

### Recommended Pricing Architecture

| Plan       | Bengali Name  | Price | Duration | Who It's For                   |
| ---------- | ------------- | ----- | -------- | ------------------------------ |
| Free       | ফ্রি          | ৳0    | Forever  | Trial + retention              |
| Exam Ready | এক্সাম রেডি   | ৳149  | 30 days  | Last-minute buyers (T-30 days) |
| Pro        | প্রো          | ৳349  | 90 days  | Serious prep students          |
| Session    | সেশন (Annual) | ৳599  | 365 days | All-in annual (best LTV)       |

### Price Anchoring

Always show yearly plan first with **"৳1.64/day"** framing. Coaches charge ৳500/class — you cost ৳1.64/day. Make that comparison explicit.

### Free Tier Design: Generous but Frustrating in the Right Places

| Feature                          | Free                                                        |
| -------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| Exams per week                   | ✅ 5 (enough to feel value)                                 |
| Score + wrong answers            | ✅ Shown                                                    |
| AI explanation                   | ❌ Locked (they'll want this after getting something wrong) |
| Chapter-wise weak point analysis | ❌ Locked (they can feel they need it)                      |
| OMR scan                         | ❌ Locked                                                   |
| Bookmark                         | ❌ Teased but locked                                        |

**The psychological trigger**: Student practices, gets 60%, wants to know _why_ they got questions wrong. That's the exact moment you show:

> "AI ব্যাখ্যা দেখতে Pro-তে Upgrade করো।"

They're emotionally engaged at that moment — highest conversion probability.

### Feature Gate Matrix

| Feature            | Free         | Exam Ready | Pro       | Session   |
| ------------------ | ------------ | ---------- | --------- | --------- |
| Daily exams        | 5/week       | Unlimited  | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| AI explanation     | First 3 only | ✓          | ✓         | ✓         |

## 6. Growth Strategy: 0 → 50,000 Users

### Phase 1: 0 → 500 users (Month 1–2) — Manual Hustle

- Go to Facebook groups for "HSC 2026 batch", "Medical admission 2026" — groups with 100K+ members
- Post helpful content (free MCQ sets, tips), **not ads**
- Find 10 "champion students" — give them free Pro access, ask for honest feedback and to post their results
- Partner with ONE coaching teacher — give them white-label access, they recommend to students
- **Goal: 500 real users who actually practice daily**

### Phase 2: 500 → 5,000 users (Month 2–6) — Content + Community

- Build a Telegram group ("Obhyash Community") — daily question of the day, students discuss
- TikTok/YouTube Shorts: "এই প্রশ্নটা কতজন পারবে?" — video with an MCQ. Viral format.
- Referral program: "বন্ধুকে invite করো, দুজনেই 30 দিন ফ্রি পাবে" — viral loop
- Target coaching center boards with QR code flyers
- **Goal: 200 paid subscribers**

### Phase 3: 5,000 → 50,000 (Month 6–18) — Retention + Virality

- Leaderboard competitions tied to real exams (prize: subscription)
- "আমার Obhyash Score" shareable card (result share card — already built ✅)
- Teacher affiliate program: teachers promote, get cut of subscriptions
- School/coaching B2B deals: bulk licenses
- **Goal: 2,000 paid subscribers, ৳1M+ ARR**

## 7. Missing Features for a Strong V1 Launch

Ranked by **impact-to-effort ratio**:

### 🔴 Critical (Launch Blockers)

1. **Automated Payment (bKash/Nagad API)**
   - Manual approval kills conversion
   - A student who pays at 11pm won't wait till next morning for admin approval
   - This alone can **3× your conversion rate**
   - bKash developer API: [developer.bka.sh](https://developer.bka.sh) has sandbox docs

2. **Onboarding Flow — Exam Target Selection**
   - "তুমি কোন পরীক্ষার জন্য প্রস্তুতি নিচ্ছ?" (HSC / MBBS / BUET)
   - Then show a personalized dashboard per target
   - Currently it's generic — first impression matters enormously

3. **Daily Question Push Notification**
   - One question per day
   - Students open, answer, see if they're right
   - Habit loop = retention driver #1

### 🟡 High Impact (First 30 Days After Launch)

4. **Exam Countdown Widget**
   - "MBBS 2026 admission: 87 দিন বাকি" at top of dashboard
   - Creates daily urgency

5. **"আজকের লক্ষ্য" (Today's Goal) Widget**
   - Gamified daily target: "আজ ২০টি প্র্যাকটিস সম্পন্ন করো" with progress ring
   - Duolingo-style habit formation

6. **Chapter/Topic Progress Map**
   - "Physics: Mechanics 80%, Optics 40%, Thermodynamics 12%"
   - Shows students where they're weakest
   - Huge retention tool AND upgrade trigger

7. **Wrong Answer Review Mode**
   - After any exam, students can practice only their wrong answers
   - Essential for serious students

### 🟢 Medium Priority (Month 2–3)

8. **Teacher-Curated "Important MCQ" Collections**
   - Handpicked 100-question packs per chapter with human curation stamp
   - Adds trust signal

9. **Friend Challenge**
   - "বন্ধুকে চ্যালেঞ্জ করো" sends a link, friend takes the same exam, scores compared
   - Viral + social

10. **Bookmark + Flashcard Review**
    - Bookmark hard questions, review them later
    - Students love this feature

### ⚪ Nice-to-Have (V2)

- AI doubt solver ("এই প্রশ্নটা বুঝিনি" → AI explains step by step)
- Video explanation clips (30-60 second shorts per tricky concept)
- Parent progress report email
- Offline mode for exam practice
